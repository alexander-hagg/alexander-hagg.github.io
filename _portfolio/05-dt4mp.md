## DigitalTwin-4-Multiphysics Lab

The **DT4MP Lab** addresses both urban digital twins (UDZ) and industrial multi-physics twins, treating them as synergistic research areas. By combining real-time data streams with advanced multi-physics simulation and AI, the lab develops tools that improve urban quality of life and industrial production efficiency.

### My role

I co-lead the DT4MP Lab, with responsibility for the **urban digital twins** pillar — in particular the [OpenSKIZZE](/portfolio/openskizze/) project on AI-assisted urban climate planning. DT4MP provides the broader research infrastructure and cross-institute collaborations that make this work possible.

### Lab focus areas

- **Urban digital twins** — modelling urban infrastructure, green spaces, and climate for city planning
- **Industrial multi-physics twins** — optimising manufacturing processes and part quality through simulation
- **AI + simulation** — combining machine learning with physics-based CFD and FEM models
- **Knowledge transfer** — providing data analysis, virtual testing, and validation services to SMEs
