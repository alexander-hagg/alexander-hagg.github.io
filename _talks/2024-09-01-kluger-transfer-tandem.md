Tandem talk on how AI methods — in particular surrogate models and generative tools — support urban planners in understanding and communicating the effects of climate change on local air quality. Part of the KLUGER Transfer initiative bridging research at H-BRS and the Max Planck Institute for Chemistry with policymakers and the public.
